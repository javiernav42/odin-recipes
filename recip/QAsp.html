Here are the ingredients needed:

  • olive oil
  • minced garlic
  • trimmed and cut Asparagus
  • salt
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add asparagus and stir-fry until soft but still firm, 5 to 7 minutes. Season with salt and lemon juice.
